# Análisis censal del pueblo Urus del Lago Poopó, Bolivia

As part of an ongoing investigative report in collaboration with [Latinas por el Clima](https://www.latinasporelclima.org/acerca-de), here we explore the socioeconomic impacts of Lake Poopó's disappeareance on the life of the indigenous community "Urus del Lago Poopó" using microdata from the **2024 Bolivian Population and Housing Census (CPV 2024)**,
published by the Instituto Nacional de Estadística (INE).

## Data

The raw CPV 2024 microdata and variable dictionaries were obtained from [here](https://www.ine.gob.bo/index.php/censos-y-banco-de-datos/censos/).
